# Legal and Compliance Cautions

This project is for private research, paper trading, risk monitoring, and tightly controlled future self-directed execution.

Do not use it for:

- Laundering or sanctions evasion
- Market manipulation
- Spoofing, layering, or wash trading
- Insider information
- Managing outside money
- Circumventing venue, jurisdictional, or tax obligations

Before any live trading work, add venue terms review, jurisdiction-specific legal review, tax/export logs, full persistence, authentication, immutable audit storage, operational runbooks, and independent kill-switch verification.
